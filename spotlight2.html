Playing Cards App

Spring 2024 - NMD Interactive II - Professor Mike Minerva

For this project we designed seven playing cards and prototyped an "app" in Figma. I themed my cards around music, so the face cards represent sections of a concert band.

This was my first time prototyping - I went from not knowing what a frame was to having a complete prototype in just a few weeks. I'm proud of what I accomplished, but I've come a long way since this project.
